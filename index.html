The Health Benefits of Singing

Singing has always been one of my favorite things to do. For me, singing is a way to relax. Besides relaxing, there are other physical, phsycological, and emotional benefits that comes from singing on a regular basis.

Physical Benefits of Singing

Singing can be an excellent form of exercise. Your lungs will get a workout as you employ proper singing techniques and vocal projections. Singing also strengthens your diaphram. While singing, standing up straight is a part of correct technique which will improve your posture.

Phsycological and Emotonal Benefits

Singing is a natural anti-depressant. When you sing, your brain releases endorphins, a chemical in your brain that makes you feel happy or uplifted. In addition, scientist have found an organ in the ear called sacculus that responds to the frequencies created by singing. The response creates a feeling of pleasure, regardless of what the singing sounds like.
